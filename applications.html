Stone Applications

Versatile, durable, and aesthetically stunning natural limestone designed for world-class architectural projects.

Outdoor Paving & Landscaping

Our naturally split, hand-cut, and tumbled limestones are extensively utilized in premium UK and European gardens. The slip-resistant surfaces provide excellent safety, while the durability ensures longevity even in freezing climates.

  • Patio and Garden Paving
  • Driveways and Walkways
  • Pool Surrounds

Indoor Flooring

With honed and polished finishes, our limestones bring warmth and luxury to residential interiors. The consistent 22mm calibration allows for seamless indoor-to-outdoor transitions, a popular trend in modern architecture.

  • Kitchen and Bathroom Flooring
  • Living Spaces
  • Feature Walls

Commercial Spaces

Engineered to withstand heavy foot traffic, our heavy-duty limestone slabs are the choice material for luxury hotels, malls, and corporate offices, particularly in the UAE and USA markets.

  • Hotel Lobbies
  • Public Plazas
  • Commercial Facades
