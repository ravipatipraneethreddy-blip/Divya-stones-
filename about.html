About Divya Stones

Established in 2002 in Bethamcherla, India, we are a direct manufacturer and bulk exporter of premium natural limestone.

Direct from factory in India with consistent color sorting for premium bulk supply.

Our Manufacturing Strength

At Divya Stones, we don't just trade stone; we manufacture it. Operating directly from our modern processing setup in Bethamcherla, we employ 50–75 highly skilled workers dedicated to precision cutting, rigorous calibration, and diverse finishing techniques.

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery enabling us to deliver 40+ containers per month consistently without compromising on quality. Every slab undergoes strict quality control to ensure uniform color sorting and precise dimensions required for international standards.
